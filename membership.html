Common Questions

What do I need to bring?

Boxing gloves, hand wraps, and shin guards. Bring water and a towel.

I've never done Muay Thai before — is it okay to join?

Absolutely. All levels are welcome. Our sessions are structured to accommodate beginners while still challenging experienced fighters.

How do I cancel my membership?

Contact Luke directly. Your membership stays active until the end of the current billing period — no penalties, no hassle.
